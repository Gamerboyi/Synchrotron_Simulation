ℹ️ What is this? ▶

This is a particle accelerator — a machine that uses electromagnetic fields to push charged particles to incredible speeds.

The green ring is the beam pipe where 40 protons orbit. The yellow structure is the RF cavity — it gives particles an energy kick each lap.

The red dot leads the beam. As particles gain energy, the magnetic field must increase to keep them on track — just like at CERN's Large Hadron Collider.

Try it: Toggle "Focus" ON to keep the beam tight. Toggle "RF Kick" OFF to stop acceleration and watch the beam coast.
